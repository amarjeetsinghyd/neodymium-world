Cookie Policy

Last updated: June 19, 2026. Understand how we use cookies, telemetry, and tracking parameters.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your computer or mobile device by your web browser when you visit a website. They allow the site to remember your actions, preferences, and session status over a period of time, so you don't have to keep re-entering them whenever you return to the site or browse from one page to another.

2. How Neodymium Uses Cookies

We use cookies to maintain session parameters and analyze aggregate traffic trends. Specifically:

  • Essential Cookies: These cookies are critical to render our layout correctly and keep track of your navigation actions (such as closing notices or submitting contact forms).
  • Analytics Cookies: We utilize basic analytics parameters (such as referral tracking) to analyze how users arrive at our intelligence briefings and which reports receive the most engagement. This helps us optimize our reporting schedule.
  • Advertising Cookies (Google AdSense): We may work with Google AdSense and third-party ad networks to display advertisements. These partners use cookies to serve personalized ads based on your previous web browsing history.

3. Types of Cookies We Use

We classify cookies under the following categories:

  • Session Cookies: Temporary cookies that remain in your browser's cookie file until you leave the Site. They help coordinate basic interface variables.
  • Persistent Cookies: These remain in your browser's cookie file for much longer (depending on the lifetime of the specific cookie). They remember settings like light/dark interface modes.
  • Third-Party Cookies: Set by domains other than neodymium.world, primarily Google AdSense for interest-based ad matching.

4. How to Manage and Opt-Out of Cookies

You can choose to accept or decline cookies. Most web browsers automatically accept cookies, but you can usually modify your browser settings to decline cookies if you prefer. However, disabling cookies may prevent you from taking full advantage of the Site's interactive features.

  • To opt-out of Google's interest-based advertising cookies, visit the Google Ads Settings Panel.
  • For comprehensive guides on how to clear or block cookies across major browsers, visit All About Cookies.

5. Contact Support

If you have any questions or require clarification regarding our Cookie Policy, please contact our support desk at neodymium.world@gmail.com.
