Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 19, 2026. Review our commitment to security and user data privacy.

1. Introduction

Welcome to Neodymium ("we," "us," or "our"). We operate Neodymium, accessible via https://neodymium.world (the "Site"). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting any personal data we process about you. This Privacy Policy details how we collect, store, share, and protect your information when you visit our Site, subscribe to our intelligence briefs, or interact with our digital dashboards.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information to provide high-quality services and strategic insights. The categories of information we collect include:

  • Personal Information: When you subscribe to our newsletter or register for access, you may provide your name, email address, job title, and organization.
  • Log Data: As is standard for news websites, our servers automatically collect log information sent by your browser. This includes your IP address, browser type, operating system, referring pages, visited pages, and the date/time of your visits.
  • Cookies and Tracking Technologies: We use cookies to verify sessions, track interface preferences, and analyze aggregate traffic trends. Read Section 4 for details on advertising cookies.

3. How We Use Your Information

We process your personal information based on our legitimate interest in providing high-value intelligence reports and maintaining the Site. Specifically, we use it to:

  • Deliver our regular Intelligence Briefing newsletters.
  • Optimize the performance and user experience of our analytical dashboards.
  • Monitor the security and integrity of our systems.
  • Comply with applicable legal requirements.

4. Third-Party Advertisers & Cookies (Google AdSense)

We may display third-party advertisements on our Site to support our operations. In doing so:

  • Google AdSense & DoubleClick Cookie: Google, as a third-party vendor, uses cookies to serve ads on our Site. Google's use of the DoubleClick DART cookie enables it and its partners to serve ads to our users based on their visit to our Site and other sites on the Internet.
  • You may opt-out of the use of the DART cookie by visiting the Google Ad and Content Network Privacy Policy.
  • Other third-party ad networks may also use cookies, web beacons, or tracking pixels to measure ad effectiveness. We do not have access to or control over cookies used by these third-party advertisers.

5. GDPR and CCPA Privacy Rights

Depending on your location, you may have specific rights regarding your personal data under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) or California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA):

  • Access and Rectification: You have the right to request access to and correction of your personal data.
  • Erasure ("Right to be Forgotten"): You may request that we delete your email subscription or personal profile details.
  • Opt-Out of Data Sale: Neodymium does not sell your personal data.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the details provided in Section 8.

6. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures (such as SSL encryption and access controls) to prevent unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction of your personal data. However, please be aware that no transmission over the Internet is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Third-Party Links

Our intelligence reports contain links to external sites (defense publications, news agencies, governmental portals). We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of these third-party websites.
